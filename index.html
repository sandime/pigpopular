📖 About the Book

Pig Popular has it all: a best friend, red sneakers that fit perfectly, and an undefeated surfing record at Piggington Beach. But when Mimsy Sage—a flashy new pig from the legendary surf spot Pigmaui—rolls onto the beach in a sparkly van wearing platform boots and claiming to know the famous Pig Duke, everything changes.

Pig Popular is a funny, warmhearted chapter book exploring friendship, belonging, and the quiet courage it takes to resist peer pressure.

📏 ~6,500 words
👧 Ages 6–9
📚 Chapter Book
